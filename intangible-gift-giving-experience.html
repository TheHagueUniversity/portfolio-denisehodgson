- intangible gift-giving experience -

During my first year studying User Experience Design at The Hague University of Applied Sciences, I got the opportunity to work with Hallmark. I got paired up with one of my classmates to tackle the project. We were assigned to design an intangible gift-giving experience that contained three parts; a card personalization tool, a personalized card, and a digital unwrapping.


I was in charge of creating the prototype for the digital unwrapping part of the gift-giving experience. I created the digital prototype of the digital unwrapping with Adobe XD. Play the video on the right to view the prototype.
